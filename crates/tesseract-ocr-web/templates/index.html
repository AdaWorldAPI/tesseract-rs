Private, loopback, and link-local addresses are refused (SSRF guard); 10 MB / 10 s cap.

Best on single text-line images at the model height (36 px); larger pages are line-found and recognized top-to-bottom. PNG, JPEG, WebP, TIFF, GIF, BMP, and PNM (PGM/PPM) are accepted.

Printed text only. The bundled eng model is trained on printed fonts — handwriting is not supported and produces garbled output. Every result now shows a confidence score, and a low score is flagged, so unreadable input (handwriting, low resolution) is called out rather than returned silently.

Result is served back as text with a download link and recognition stats.
